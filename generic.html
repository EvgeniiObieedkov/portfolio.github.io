My career began at PJSC "Rosseti", the largest energy company in Russia, managing over 80% of the country's power grid and ranked among the top 3 state-owned companies. In this company, I led a team focused on the diagnostics of electrical equipment, which helped me develop strong coordination skills and the ability to solve complex problems under high responsibility. I also analyzed data on equipment status, marking my first step into the world of data analytics.
I then moved to Kvantron Group LLC, a leader in Russia's industrial automation market and a Skolkovo resident, working with major manufacturing and energy companies. As part of the implementation of the national "Chestny znak" labeling system, I was responsible for installing and maintaining technical systems. This experience allowed me to handle large data volumes and real-time analytics, which deepened my interest in data analysis and process automation.
Later, I worked in industrial electronics manufacturing, focusing on process optimization, workflow improvements, and automation initiatives. I applied data analysis to increase throughput, reduce lead times, and improve reporting efficiency.
Next, I joined TOO Digital System Servis, a leading Kazakhstani company specializing in automation and digital solutions for industry. There, I managed the launch of new production lines and the modernization of equipment. Despite the technical focus, I actively applied data to optimize processes and improve production efficiency. I worked on analyzing production metrics and implementing automation, which helped reduce labor costs and boost productivity.
My experience in data analytics has shown me how essential accurate analysis and automation are for achieving business goals. I now aim to apply my expertise in areas where data plays a key role in optimizing business processes, analyzing customer behavior, and enhancing financial operations.
